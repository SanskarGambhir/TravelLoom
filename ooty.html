Best Places to Visit in Ooty
Ooty Lake

This artificial lake is a popular tourist spot where visitors can enjoy boating amidst scenic surroundings. The lake offers stunning views of the lush greenery and is an ideal place for picnics and leisurely walks.
Doddabetta Peak

Situated at an altitude of 2,637 meters, Doddabetta is the highest peak in the Nilgiri Hills. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys, tea estates, and distant hills. There's a telescope house at the summit for better views.
Emerald Lake

Located near Avalanche, about 25 kilometers from Ooty, Emerald Lake is a serene reservoir surrounded by lush greenery and dense forests. It's an ideal spot for nature walks, birdwatching, and photography.
